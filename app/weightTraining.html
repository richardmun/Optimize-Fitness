WEIGHT TRAINING

Physical fitness is a state of health and well-being. The ability to perform aspects of sports, occupations and daily activities. Physical fitness is generally achieved through proper nutrition, moderate-vigorous physical exercise, and sufficient rest.

Chest

Target Muscle Group

Bench Press

Strength-training that involves laying on your back and lifting barbell upward from your chest, targeting the muscles of the overall chest, shoulders, and triceps.

Dumbbell Incline Press

Strength-training that involves laying on your back on an incline and lifting a pair of dumbbells upward from your chest, targeting the muscles of the uppermost chest, shoulders, and triceps.

Cable Chest Fly

Resistance-training movement that primarily isolates the chest muscles to develop strength and definition in the chest.

Pushups

Fundamental bodyweight exercise that targets the chest, shoulders, triceps, and core muscles by pushing chest away from the surface.



Back

Target Muscle Group

Barbell Row

Strength-training that focuses on the upper back, including the latissimus dorsi, rhmboids, and traps, as well the lower back, biceps, and forearms.

T-Bar Row

Strength-training that targets the upper and lower back which creates definition when maintaining tension throughout the movement.

Pullups

Effective bodyweight exercise that utilizes the upper body, such as back and arms, to develop strength and muscles.
