Cardio

A high-intensity interval training, that improves strength and conditioning by performing functional movements with explosiveness.

Exercises

Stairmaster

Action of climbing stairs provides an intense workout that can help improve cardiovascular health, endurance, and leg strength. This high-intense exercise targets lower body muscles like the quadriceps, hamstrings, calves, and glutes.

Sprints
Short bursts of maximum effort running ranging from 50 to 400 meters in distance. Improves cardiovascular fitness, builds muscular strength and power, enhances anaerobic conditioning, promotes fat loss, boosts speed and agility, stimulates hormonal responses, and develops mental toughness.
Rows
Rows can be completed in a circuit-style workout, incorporating them with other cardio exercises with minimal rest between sets. This approach would help maintain an elevated heart rate throughout the workout, providing both strength training and cardiovascular benefits.
Jump Rope
Using jump rope promotes cardiovascular health by elevating heart rate and blood circulation which burns significant calories in short amount of time. Other benefits include improved coordination and agility, strengthening of bone density and joints, and engagement of muscles which can improve muscle tone, strength, and endurance.
