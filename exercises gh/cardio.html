Cardio Fitness

Cardio fitness consists of rhythmic activity that raises your heart rate into your target heart rate zone. This is the zone where you burn the most fat and calories.

Variations of Cardio Exercises

Treadmill

Treadmill is a form of cardio that consists of running at a set pace. You can run fast or slow depending on the speed that you can maintain.
