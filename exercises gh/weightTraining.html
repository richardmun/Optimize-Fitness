Weight Training

Maximize your strength and lean muscle mass through intense weight training exercises. These various exercises are capable of increasing your muscle fibers by breaking down your body.

Exercises Based on Muscle Target Group

Target Group: Chest

Bench Press

The bench press is a compound exercise that targets the muscles of the upper body. It involves lying on a bench and pressing weight upward. During a bench press, you lower the weight down to chest level and then press upwards while extending your arms and repeat.


Dumbbell Incline Press

Incline press involves adjusting the bench to an incline of 15-30 degrees and then lie back, engage core, and press the dumbbells towards the celing. Slowly lower the weight until your reach a 90 degree bend at the elbows and repeat.


Cable Chest Fly

Cables flies involves setting both pulleys at shoulder height, holding both handles with neutral grip, with one foot infront. Start with arms forward with bent elbows to keep tension while flexing your chest. Slowly open arms wide by focusing on your shoulder joints while flexing your chest. Then, return to starting by position by pushing handles together while continuing to flex your chest and repeat.
